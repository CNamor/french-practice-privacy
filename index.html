Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

French Practice does not require an account or any personal information. This policy explains what data is collected to keep the app free and improve your experience.

Information we collect

Analytics: anonymous usage data (screens viewed, features used) processed by PostHog on EU servers. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Crash reports: if the app crashes, a diagnostic report (device type, OS version, stack trace) is sent to Sentry so we can fix bugs.

Advertising: the app displays adverts via Google AdMob. If you consent to tracking, AdMob may use a device identifier to show relevant adverts. If you decline, only contextual adverts are shown.

Information we do not collect

We do not collect your name, email, location, photos, contacts, or any other personal information. Your practice progress, game scores, and preferences are stored locally on your device and never leave it.

Third-party services

  • Google AdMob — advertising (device identifiers with consent, ad interaction data).
  • PostHog — analytics (anonymous usage events, EU servers).
  • Sentry — crash reporting (device type, OS version, crash diagnostics).

Your choices

You can decline ad tracking when prompted by iOS, and change this at any time in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking. You can reset your practice progress from the app's Progress screen.

Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at guarijewellery@gmail.com.
